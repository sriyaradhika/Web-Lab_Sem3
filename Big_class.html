Mahendra Singh Dhoni 

Introduction about MS DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( born 7 July 1981), is a former Indian international cricketer who captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. MS Dhoni is the only captain in the history of cricket to win all ICC trophies.


One Day Internationals Dhoni is one of the highest run scorers with more than 10,000 runs scored and is considered an effective 'finisher' in limited-overs formats.
